Our Purpose

We are a group of individuals dedicated to helping students identify the best steps to take in achieving their desired career aspirations. By considering different educational pairings and backgrounds, we aim to create a platform that offers personalized guidance and insights. Using Generative AI, we enhance this process by automating analysis, offering tailored recommendations, and providing innovative solutions to students' career planning challenges.

Objective

Our objective is to foster a more inclusive and supportive guide for prospective applicants and current students of all backgrounds for Smeal's Masters' program.
